Health Data & Integrations

PillThinker supports user-authorized health data connections. Users remain in control of what information is shared with PillThinker.

Supported and Planned Integrations

  • Epic / MyChart (SMART on FHIR)
  • Additional healthcare systems as they become available

What Information May Be Accessed

  • Medications
  • Medication Requests
  • Allergies
  • Conditions
  • Patient Demographics
  • Health Records and Observations when available

User Authorization

PillThinker does not access health information unless the user explicitly authorizes the connection through the healthcare organization's authentication and authorization process.

Revoking Access

Users may revoke access through available account settings, healthcare portal settings, or by contacting support.

Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of imported health data by contacting support@getpillthinker.com.

Important Notice

PillThinker is not a healthcare provider and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Users should verify imported information with their healthcare provider.
