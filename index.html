Health Data

Built for user-authorized health data connections

PillThinker is preparing support for patient-authorized health data connections, including Epic/MyChart through SMART on FHIR. Users must approve access before supported health information can be imported.

  • Medication and medication request information
  • Allergies and conditions
  • Patient demographics and health records when available
  • User-controlled connection and revocation options

Designed around consent and transparency

PillThinker will only access connected health data after the user authorizes the connection through the supported healthcare system flow. Imported information should always be reviewed and confirmed with a licensed healthcare professional.

Important medical notice

PillThinker is not a healthcare provider and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Medication information, AI explanations, reminders, imported records, and interaction warnings are informational tools only. Always confirm health decisions with a physician, pharmacist, or qualified healthcare professional.
