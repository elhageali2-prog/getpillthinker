How PillThinker works

The app is designed to feel simple from the first day, while still supporting more advanced health record features.

Add or import medication information

Users can enter medications manually and, where supported, connect authorized health data sources.

Organize reminders and records

Medication schedules, refill details, health logs, allergies, and conditions stay grouped together.

Review before sharing

Users stay in control and can review selected information before sending updates to a provider.

Health Data

Patient-authorized health data connections

PillThinker supports a consent-first approach to connected health data and is expanding compatibility with healthcare systems, including Epic/MyChart through SMART on FHIR.

  • Medication and medication request information
  • Allergies, conditions, and health records when available
  • User review before imported data is used inside the app
  • Clear support for data access and deletion requests

Consent and transparency first

PillThinker only accesses connected health data when a user authorizes that connection. Users should always verify imported information with their healthcare provider or pharmacist.

Provider and clinic tools

Provider features help clinics organize patient-selected updates and communicate more clearly with linked patients.

Provider dashboard

Clinic accounts can use a dedicated dashboard for patient update workflows.

Patient-selected updates

Patients choose what medication, health log, or history information they want to share.

Secure account access

Provider accounts are designed with managed access and support channels.

Important medical notice

PillThinker is not a healthcare provider and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Medication information, AI explanations, reminders, imported records, and interaction warnings are informational tools only. Always confirm health decisions with a physician, pharmacist, or qualified healthcare professional.
