Health Data & Integrations

PillThinker may support user-authorized health data connections, including Epic/MyChart through SMART on FHIR and future integrations such as NextGen. Users must authorize access before PillThinker can import supported health information. Imported data may be used to help populate medication lists, allergies, conditions, and health records inside the app.

PillThinker is not a healthcare provider and does not replace medical advice. Users should verify imported information with their healthcare provider or pharmacist.
